Studio at Butler

Image by: @elotroalex

Spring 2019 Open Labs (Jan 22 – May 6)
  • Mondays and Fridays, 3:00 pm – 5:00 pm

Studio at Butler offers space for between 25 and 40 participants in room 208B of Butler Library. During Open Labs, the space makes use of wheeled tables and whiteboards to create small islands of collaborative activity. The room, with a tall vaulted ceiling, has ten separate tables that can be easily reconfigured.

Studio at Butler has no computers onsite, but it does have a large television to which users can connect their laptops via HDMI.

Getting to Studio at Butler

  1. Take the ❶ to 116th St. Columbia University.
  2. Cut midway across campus. With the Alma Mater statue to your left, turn right toward Butler Library.
  3. Upon entering Butler and passing through access control, turn left.
  4. Go around the center of the building to room 208B.
