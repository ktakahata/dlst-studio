Studio Recommended Software

While the Columbia University Libraries Studio practice does not pursue a strictly tool-oriented approach to digital, data-driven research and pedagogy, we find that our goals of ethical, sustainable, collaborative, and FAIR work align best with software that is free and/or open source as well as with file formats that are open and as flat as possible. To that end, we recommend that our collaborators—especially beginners—install and use the following applications on their computers.
